About the project

Open source beigel is an attempt at providing the internet with the means to create, share and contribute to the development of the beigel. To freely, without restriction, use the recipes found here for whatever they want.

The world of beigels is a glorous one and we at Open source Beigel wish to share it with everyone. There is nothing better than a freshly baked beigel.
